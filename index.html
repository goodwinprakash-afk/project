Elyon

Meaning of Elyon
• Hebrew Origin: The word ʿElyōn comes from the Hebrew root meaning “high,” “upper,” or “supreme.”
• Biblical Usage: It is most often paired with El (God), forming El Elyon = “God Most High.”
Translation: In Greek (Septuagint), it is rendered as ho Theos ho hypsistos (“God the Highest”).
